About Us

Nestled along the scenic Nilaveli to Trincomalee road, Panorama Safari Villa offers an unforgettable wildlife experience in Sri Lanka. Our villa combines luxury accommodation with thrilling safari tours to bring you closer to the island's majestic leopards, elephants, and crocodiles.
